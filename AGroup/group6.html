2.M hnh Fama-French 3 nhn t:

\(r_i=alpha_i+beta1_i*(r_M-r_f)+beta2_i*SMB+beta3_i*HML+e_i\)

\(r_i\) : t sut sinh li ti sn i

\(r_M\) : t sut sinh li th trng

\(r_f\) : li sut phi ri ro

\(SMB\) : nhn t quy m vn ho(Size Factor)

\(HML\) : nhn t ch s gi tr ghi s/gi tr th trng(Book to Market)

\(beta1_i;beta2_i;beta3_i\) : c k vng l dng

-Vn t ra by gi l khi ng dng vo 50 c phiu trn sn giao dch chng khon H Ni, ba nhn t trong m hnh Fama-French c tht s gii thch c s thay i t sut sinh li c phiu.

-C hay khng nhng cng ty c quy m nh s cho t sut sinh li cao hn nhng cng ty c quy m ln v vic nhng cng ty c t s Book/Market cao s cho t sut sinh li cao hn nhng cng ty c t s ny thp nh Fama-French pht biu.

Vn ny s c tr li khi ta tip tc thc hin cc bc phn tch sau y.
